Launch Your Business on a Solid Foundation

Starting a business is an exciting journey, but navigating the complexities of company formation, legal structures, licensing requirements, and operational planning can be overwhelming — especially in unfamiliar markets. BeSURE's Business Setup & Consulting service provides comprehensive, hands-on guidance to entrepreneurs and companies looking to establish or expand operations in the United States, India, or across international markets. We handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on building your vision.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in business incorporation, regulatory compliance, financial structuring, and market analysis. We understand the nuances of different business structures — from LLCs and corporations to partnerships and sole proprietorships — and we help you choose the right entity type that aligns with your goals, tax considerations, and liability preferences. Beyond formation, we provide the strategic consulting needed to set your business up for long-term success from day one.

What We Deliver

  • Company formation and incorporation across US states and Indian jurisdictions
  • Business entity selection consulting (LLC, Corporation, LLP, Partnership, Sole Proprietorship)
  • Business licensing and permit acquisition for industry-specific requirements
  • EIN/Tax ID registration and state tax registration setup
  • Comprehensive business plan development with financial projections
  • Market research and competitive analysis for new market entry
  • Operational setup including banking, accounting systems, and vendor relationships
  • Office space planning, virtual office setup, and registered agent services
  • Franchise consulting and franchise agreement review
  • Cross-border business structuring for US-India operations

Our Approach

BeSURE follows a systematic, milestone-driven approach to business setup that ensures nothing is overlooked and your launch timeline stays on track:

Phase 1 — Consultation & Planning: We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your business concept, target market, budget, and timeline. We assess the regulatory landscape, recommend the optimal business structure, and create a comprehensive setup roadmap with clear milestones and deliverables.

Phase 2 — Formation & Registration: Our team handles all legal paperwork, from articles of incorporation and operating agreements to EIN applications and state registrations. We coordinate with legal counsel to ensure all documents are properly drafted and filed, and we manage follow-up with government agencies to expedite approvals.

Phase 3 — Operational Infrastructure: Beyond paperwork, we help you build the operational backbone of your business. This includes setting up business bank accounts, implementing accounting and payroll systems, establishing vendor relationships, securing necessary insurance, and configuring the technology stack you need to operate efficiently.

Phase 4 — Launch Support & Growth Planning: Once your business is operational, we provide ongoing consulting to help you navigate the critical first months. We assist with hiring strategy, marketing launch plans, financial management, and growth planning to ensure you build momentum and establish a strong market presence from the outset.

Who This Service Is For

Our Business Setup & Consulting service is designed for first-time entrepreneurs turning their ideas into registered businesses, international companies expanding into the US or Indian markets, existing businesses restructuring or forming new subsidiaries, franchise operators setting up new locations, professionals transitioning from employment to business ownership, and investors establishing holding companies or special purpose vehicles.
